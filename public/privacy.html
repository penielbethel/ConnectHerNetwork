Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 2025

ConnectHer Network ("we", "our", "us") provides communication features including audio and video calls, messaging, and community interactions. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account and Profile: Name, email, phone (if provided), and profile details.
  • Communications: Messages, call metadata (time, duration, participants). We do not store audio/video content of live calls.
  • Device and Usage: App version, device model, OS, logs, and analytics events to improve functionality.
  • Camera and Microphone: Access is requested at runtime when you start video/audio features. Media is used only to provide the requested functionality.
  • Notifications: Push tokens to deliver notifications.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve app features like calls, messaging, and communities.
  • Secure the platform, prevent abuse, and support users.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Data Sharing

  • With service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, push notifications) bound by contracts.
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • With your consent.

Permissions

  • Camera: For video calls, capturing photos/videos when you choose to share.
  • Microphone: For audio/video calls and voice messages.
  • Storage/Media: Saving and sharing media you choose.
  • Notifications: Delivering updates and call alerts.
  • Network/Phone State: Connectivity checks and call handling.
  • Location (if enabled): Optional features that may require approximate location.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide services and comply with legal or operational requirements. You may request deletion subject to applicable laws and platform constraints.

Your Choices

  • Manage permissions in your device settings at any time.
  • Control notifications via in-app settings and device OS.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data.

Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Transfers

Your information may be stored and processed in countries where we or our providers operate, with safeguards in place.

Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding this policy, contact: support@connecther.network.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The effective date will reflect the latest changes. Significant updates will be communicated via the app or website.
